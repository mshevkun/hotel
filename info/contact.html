Contact Hudson Valley Resort & Spa

Getting Here

By Car

From the South

Take the New York State Thruway (I-87) north to Exit 18 – New Paltz. Turn left onto Route 299 and continue for 6 miles to the end. Turn right onto Route 44/55 and continue for 8.5 miles. Turn right at the sign for Hudson Valley Resort & Spa onto Lower Granite Road. Continue for 1 mile to the stop sign and turn left. The resort is 0.5 miles ahead on the left.

You can also take I-84 to the New York State Thruway (I-87), then follow the directions above.

From the North

Take the New York State Thruway (I-87) south to Exit 19 – Kingston. Follow Route 28 (Pine Hill) to Route 209 south (Ellenville). Continue 18 miles and turn left onto County Route 27 in Accord. Go 0.2 miles and turn right onto Granite Road. The resort is 2.3 miles ahead on the right.

I-90 connects to the New York State Thruway (I-87); then follow the directions above.

From the East

Take Route 17 west (future I-86) to Exit 113 – Ellenville/Wurtsboro. Turn right onto Route 209 north. Continue 6 miles past Ellenville and turn right onto Route 44/55. Go 1.5 miles and turn left at the sign for Hudson Valley Resort & Spa onto Lower Granite Road. Continue for 1 mile to the stop sign and turn left. The resort is 0.5 miles ahead on the left.

From the West

Take Route 17 (future I-86) east to Exit 113 – Ellenville/Wurtsboro. Turn left onto Route 209 north. Continue 6 miles past Ellenville and turn right onto Route 44/55. Go 1.5 miles and turn left at the sign for Hudson Valley Resort & Spa onto Lower Granite Road. Continue for 1 mile to the stop sign and turn left. The resort is 0.5 miles ahead on the left.

By Plane

Stewart International Airport is approximately 45 minutes from the resort. Please check with your airline for current flight schedules.

By Bus

Short Line Bus offers scheduled service from the Port Authority Bus Terminal in New York City to Kerhonkson.

Short Line Bus
Tel: 800-631-8405
Please refer to their website for current schedules.

By Train

Metro-North Railroad provides scheduled service from Grand Central Terminal in New York City to nearby Poughkeepsie.

Metro-North Railroad
Website: View LIRR and Metro-North schedules
Please check their site for schedule information.

Hudson Valley Resort & Spa
Events & Attractions

Recently named a “Top 10 US Travel Destination” by Lonely Planet, there’s never been a better time to visit the Hudson Valley. Explore the exciting events and area attractions which draw so many visitors to the region year after year.

With so many opportunities for exploration, recreation, and relaxation, the Hudson Valley is truly in the midst of a travel Renaissance: Food, wine, and film festivals, a thriving art and music scene, hundreds of miles of scenic hiking and mountain biking trails, national historic sites, and a progressive focus on natural health and wellness; the Hudson Valley has something for everyone.

Upcoming Events

Check out these upcoming events! Food festivals and cultural events. Harvest celebrations and world-class music performances. The Hudson Valley Resort & Spa is your ideal choice for experiencing the best of the Hudson Valley and Catskill Region yourself.

Explore the Outdoors

The Hudson Valley Resort & Spa is just minutes from some of the most exciting trail networks, parks, and wildlife preserves in the Northeast, offering endless opportunities for outdoor adventure. Minnewaska State Park, the Mohonk Preserve, Sundown Wild Forest, Sam’s Point Preserve, and the Catskill Mountains offer visitors hundreds of miles of scenic natural landscapes to explore year-round, whether hiking, mountain-biking, or cross-country skiing.

Area Attractions

Thousands of acres of pristine forest preserves offer visitors exceptional hiking opportunities, with national museums and historical sites, shopping and fine dining just minutes away. Perfectly situated in the heart of the Shawangunk Mountains, the Hudson Valley Resort & Spa is at the center of it all!
